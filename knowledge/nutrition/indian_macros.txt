PORTION GUIDELINES
═══════════════════════════════════════

HAND-BASED PORTIONING (No scale needed):
- 1 fist = ~1 cup = ~150g cooked rice/dal
- 1 palm (size + thickness) = ~100g protein (chicken/paneer)
- 1 thumb = ~1 tbsp oil/ghee/peanut butter
- 2 cupped hands = ~1 serving vegetables
- 1 cupped hand = ~1 serving fruit/nuts

COMMON WEIGHTS:
- 1 katori (small bowl): ~150ml dal/sabzi
- 1 plate rice: ~200-250g cooked
- 1 roti: ~60g cooked (35g dry atta)
- 1 glass milk: ~200ml
- 1 tablespoon: ~15ml (oil) / ~15g (ghee/butter)
