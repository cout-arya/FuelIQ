FAT LOSS NUTRITION GUIDELINES — COMPREHENSIVE INDIAN CONTEXT
Source: ICMR/NIN + Sports Nutrition Research + Clinical Guidelines

═══════════════════════════════════════
CALORIC DEFICIT FUNDAMENTALS
═══════════════════════════════════════

CALCULATING DEFICIT:
- Target: 300-500 kcal deficit below TDEE (maintenance)
- Moderate deficit: 300-400 kcal (sustainable, minimal muscle loss)
- Aggressive deficit: 500-750 kcal (faster results, higher muscle loss risk)
- Never go below 1200 kcal (women) or 1500 kcal (men)
- Safe rate: 0.5-1 kg fat loss per week (0.5-1% body weight)

TDEE CALCULATION (Mifflin-St Jeor — Gold Standard):
Men: (10 × weight in kg) + (6.25 × height in cm) - (5 × age) + 5
Women: (10 × weight in kg) + (6.25 × height in cm) - (5 × age) - 161
Multiply by Activity Factor:
- Sedentary (desk job): × 1.2
- Lightly active (1-3 days gym): × 1.375
- Moderately active (3-5 days gym): × 1.55
- Very active (6-7 days gym): × 1.725
- Athlete/laborer: × 1.9

PLATEAU MANAGEMENT:
- If no weight loss after 2 weeks: reduce by 100-150 kcal
- If plateau persists 3+ weeks: add 1 refeed day at maintenance
- Diet breaks: 1-2 weeks at maintenance every 8-12 weeks
- Reverse dieting post-cut: add 100 kcal/week back to maintenance

MACRONUTRIENT TARGETS FOR FAT LOSS
═══════════════════════════════════════

PROTEIN (Most critical macro during deficit):
- Target: 1.8-2.4g per kg bodyweight (higher end during deficit)
- 60kg person: 108-144g protein daily
- 70kg person: 126-168g protein daily
- 80kg person: 144-192g protein daily
- 90kg person: 162-216g protein daily
Rationale: High protein preserves lean mass, increases TEF (thermic effect of food — burns 20-30% of protein calories during digestion), and reduces hunger hormones (ghrelin).

CARBOHYDRATES:
- Training days: 2-3g per kg bodyweight
- Rest days: 1.5-2g per kg bodyweight
- Prioritize complex carbs (roti > naan, brown rice > white rice)
- Time carbs around workouts for performance

FATS:
- Minimum: 0.8g per kg bodyweight (hormonal health)
- Target: 0.8-1.2g per kg bodyweight
- Never drop below 40g total fat (testosterone, estrogen regulation)
- Sources: ghee, nuts, seeds, fatty fish, olive oil

MACRO SPLIT:
- Moderate deficit: Protein 35% | Carbs 35% | Fat 30%
- Aggressive deficit: Protein 40% | Carbs 30% | Fat 30%

═══════════════════════════════════════
FOODS TO MINIMIZE / AVOID
═══════════════════════════════════════

HIGH-CALORIE TRAPS (Indian-specific):
- Fried snacks: Samosa (250 kcal), pakora (300 kcal), bhujia
- Chole bhature: 850 kcal per plate — avoid completely during cut
- Restaurant paneer dishes: 350-400 kcal per serving (cream/cashew gravies)
- Paratha with ghee: 280+ kcal each — max 1 per day
- Indian sweets: Gulab jamun (150 kcal each), jalebi, ladoo
- Sugary chai (3-4 cups): 240-320 kcal daily — switch to green tea or black
- White bread products: Pav, bun, toast — use roti instead
- Packaged juices: 100+ kcal per glass — eat whole fruit
- Soft drinks: 140 kcal per can — zero nutrition
- Biryani: 450-650 kcal per plate — save for refeed day

HIDDEN CALORIES:
- Ghee on roti: +40 kcal per roti (2 roti = +80 kcal)
- Tadka/tempering: +30-60 kcal per dish
- Restaurant cooking oil: +100-200 kcal vs home cooking
- Sugar in chai: +30 kcal per teaspoon (2 tsp × 4 cups = 240 kcal)
- Nuts overdose: 30g almonds = 170 kcal (easy to eat 100g = 570 kcal)

═══════════════════════════════════════
ADVANCED FAT LOSS STRATEGIES
═══════════════════════════════════════

MEAL TIMING:
- Front-load calories: 40% breakfast, 35% lunch, 25% dinner
- Protein with every meal: controls ghrelin (hunger hormone)
- Last meal 2-3 hours before sleep
- Intermittent fasting (16:8): eat 12pm-8pm if it suits lifestyle
  - Not mandatory but helps some people control total intake
  - Skip breakfast, have larger lunch + dinner

METABOLIC RATE PROTECTION:
- Never crash diet (slows metabolism by 15-25%)
- 1-2 refeed days per week at maintenance calories
- Prioritize resistance training over cardio (preserves muscle)
- Muscle burns 6-7 kcal/kg/day at rest vs fat at 2 kcal/kg/day
- Sleep 7-9 hours (poor sleep increases cortisol → belly fat storage)

HYDRATION:
- 35ml per kg bodyweight daily (70kg = 2.45L minimum)
- Drink 500ml water 30 min before meals (increases satiety 25-30%)
- Green tea: 2-3 cups daily (EGCG catechins boost metabolism ~3-4%)
- Black coffee (no sugar): 200-400mg caffeine/day aids fat oxidation

HUNGER MANAGEMENT:
- High-volume, low-calorie foods: lauki, tori, cucumber, watermelon
- Chew slowly: 20+ minutes per meal (signals satiety to brain)
- Smaller plates: psychological portion control (Delboeuf illusion)
- Fiber target: 25-35g daily from vegetables, dals, whole grains

TRACKING:
- Weigh daily, average weekly (eliminates water fluctuation noise)
- Waist measurement: most reliable fat loss indicator
- Progress photos every 2 weeks (same lighting, same time)
- If weight stable but waist shrinking: body recomposition (good!)

SUSTAINABILITY:
- 1 cheat meal per week (not cheat day — 1 meal ≠ 1 day)
- 80/20 rule: 80% clean eating, 20% flexibility
- Build habits: fat loss is a 12-24 week project minimum
- After reaching goal: reverse diet back to maintenance over 4-6 weeks
