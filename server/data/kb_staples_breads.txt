WHEAT BREADS (ROTI FAMILY)
═══════════════════════════════════════

Roti/Chapati (1 medium, 35g atta = 60g cooked):
Cal: 120 | P: 3.5g | C: 22g | F: 2g | Fibre: 2.5g
Notes: Whole wheat. Add 30-40 kcal if brushed with ghee. Most common Indian bread.

Cal: 85 | P: 2.5g | C: 16g | F: 1g | Fibre: 2g

PORTION & COOKING CONTEXT
═══════════════════════════════════════

HOME COOKING: Base values listed above.
RESTAURANT: Add 30-50% more calories (more oil, butter, cream).
DHABA: Add 40-60% more calories (heavy oil/ghee).
STREET FOOD: Add 20-40% more calories (deep frying).
DABBA/TIFFIN: Similar to home cooking (-5 to +10%).
CLOUD KITCHEN: Similar to restaurant (+25-40%).

COOKING METHOD CALORIE IMPACT:
- Steaming (idli, dhokla): No added calories
- Dry roasting (roti, phulka): +5% calories
- Shallow fry (paratha, dosa): +15-25% calories
- Deep fry (puri, vada, pakora): +40-60% calories
- Tandoor (naan, tandoori roti): +5% calories
- Gravy — tomato-onion base: +50-80 kcal per serving
- Gravy — cream/cashew (Mughlai): +100-180 kcal per serving
- Gravy — coconut (South/Kerala): +60-100 kcal per serving
- Tempering (tadka) per dish: +30-60 kcal (depends on oil/ghee)
